Reunion FAQ

What should I wear?

Suggested attire for the dinner on Saturday is Business Casual, but most of all, we want you to feel comfortable. The days of keeping up with the trends to impress each other are over. So wear something that you know looks good on you, and that you can feel comfortable in!

Can I bring a guest?

You are welcome to bring one guest, as long as you RSVP ahead of time, so that we can have an accurate count for meals.

What is on the agenda?

In June, you will receive a formal invitation including details and costs of the Saturday dinner, location of the casual Friday night gathering, and information on any extra activities planned for our group.

Can I attend if I didn't graduate with the rest of the class?

Most certainly! We are looking forward to catching up with you!

Where can I find a yearbook for the Class of 1970?

The reunion committee will be bringing the copies we have on hand for browsing throughout the weekend. Feel free to bring memorabilia you would like to share.
